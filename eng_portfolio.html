Website maintenance

This site is a portfolio of my abilities at the moment. Taking into account the fact that I probably have a margin of progression, the idea is to make this site evolve. That's why updates are to be expected. They will be focused on an improvement of the navigation comfort and the ergonomics of the site. It will be possible to find among others a newsletter. It is thus possible to consider this site as a logbook making share of my evolution since the day of creation of the site.

It's interesting

Interesting is a blog based on general culture. Its vocation will be to answer everyday questions that people ask themselves without systematically finding an answer. Interesting can be a help to parents who have no answer to some of their children's questions. It will also be the means to awaken the curiosity of the youngest by the presence of various and varied resources.

Wordev

Wordev is a future project requiring for my part several years of experience. I won't say that it is an ultimate project, but an important one. It aims to promote codinng around the world, and why not also raise awareness of the computer community on the subject. The outcome of this project will depend on the opportunities I get, but also on my professional and personal evolution.
